How to reach ISA

Istituto Superiore Antincendi - Via del Commercio, 13 - ROMA - Italy.

You can easily reach "Istituto Superiore Antincendi" by underground (B-line, "Piramide" station), or bus (23 or 769). It's also only 400 meters far from F.S. train station "Ostiense", which is linked with the international Fiumicino Airport.

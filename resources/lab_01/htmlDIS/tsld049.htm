Comparison of Transaction Accesses

    Compare Z(E) (i.e., weight of transactions accessing the main entity) with SUM(Z(Ei)) (i.e., the sum of weights of the transactions accessing the sub-entities)


    Given P1,...Pn transactions accessing an entity T:

    Z(T) = Si=1,..nF(Pi)*S(Pi)

    where F is the frequency and S is the priority of Pi

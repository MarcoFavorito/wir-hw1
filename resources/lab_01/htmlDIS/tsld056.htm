Notes

  • All integrity constraints arised during conceptual design and/or translation have to be imported in the relational schema (mainly as key inclusion constraints).

  • All relations have to be in 1NF (see later), i.e. all attributes are atomic and single-valued.

  • Relations obtained translating the ER schema can be further normalized (3NF or BCNF) (see later)

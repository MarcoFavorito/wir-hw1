Scientific Curriculum of Enrico Giunchiglia

For more detailed information, see Enrico Giunchiglia's web page Enrico Giunchiglia is associate professor in Computer Science at the University of Genoa and leader of the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory at DIST (Dipartimento di Informatica Sistemistica e Telematica). He is Area editorial committee of the journal "Electronic Transactions on Artificial Intelligence" and he has been Program Comittee of various international conferences in the field of Artificial Intelligence. He published on the Journal of Artificial Intelligence, IEEE Transactions on SMC, Annals of Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence, Internation Journal of Intelligent Systems, IJCAI, AAAI, KR, ECAI.

The international recognition of the achievements obtained by the AI group is shown by several national and international awards won by present and past members of the group.

  • Francesco M. Donini, Maurizio Lenzerini, Daniele Nardi, and Werner Nutt.
    Tractable Concept Languages. Best Paper Award, Proceedings of the Twelfth International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence (IJCAI'91)

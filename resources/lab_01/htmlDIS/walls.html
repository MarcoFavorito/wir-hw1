Feature Extraction: Vertical Planes



    Extracting the walls amounts to:

    1. detecting the vertical planes of the walls

    2. extracting the texture of the walls



    1. Plane extraction: 3D Hough Transform is used for detecting planes form a set of 3D points.



       



    2. Texture mapping: Given the position and orientation of a wall and the camera calibration parameters (perspective matrix), texture is extracted from the original color image.





    The image is then rectified in order to have a frontal view of the scene.This texture will be then mapped in the 3D rectangles representing the walls.

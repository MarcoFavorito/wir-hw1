Where do you come from?:

Airport Leonardo da Vinci

From here, you can take a taxi, a train or rent a car.
Taking the train, it is possible to choose a fast one for Termini station (timetable available) or a slower one for Tiburtina station.

Termini Station

From Termini station, take the underground B line in the "Laurentina" bound and get off at "Colosseo" (look at the area map).

Tiburtina Station

From Tiburtina station, take the underground B line in the "Laurentina" bound and get off at "Colosseo" (look at the area map).

Conference venue
The 45th Annual IEEE Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science (FOCS '04) will be held at:

Jolly Hotel Vittorio Veneto
Corso Italia 1, 00198 Roma (RM) Italy
Telephone: +39 06 84951 Fax: +39 06 8841104
E-mail: roma_vittorioveneto@jollyhotels.it

It is located in the very heart of the city center, just a few minutes walk from Via Veneto, the famous Dolce Vita street. We have reserved a block of rooms for FOCS 2004 participants at the Jolly Hotel and at nearby cheaper hotels: please see the registration page for further details. The FOCS social dinner will be held at the National Gallery of Modern and Contemporary Art in Rome.

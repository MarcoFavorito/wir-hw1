Foundations and Methods

We shall explore promising methodologies and design new tools both for the applications discussed in this programme and for some new areas. Research will focus on realistic models of computation, highly efficient combinatorial and optimisation algorithms, and models of randomness for algorithm design and precise performance prediction.


  • Parallel and distributed computing, and computational models
  • Combinatorial algorithms
  • Randomness and analysis of algorithms

3D Hough Transform



3D Hough Transform detects planes form a set of 3D points.

Extension of the 2D HT used for detecting lines from a set of points.



2D Hough Transform demo




No applets

Interesting features:

  1. local maxima = best fitting lines (no clustering needed)
  2. diplacement of lines in (x,y) = distance of points in (rho,th)
  3. robust to occlusions and isolated points

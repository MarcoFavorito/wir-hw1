Hough Transform Localization and Image Correlation



    Under the planar movement assumption, robot motion is described by 3 variables: 2 translations and 1 rotation on the ground plane.



    The localization task is performed in two steps:



    1. Rough Localization: Hough Transform for determining 1 translation and 1 rotation variables + Odometry for determining the other translation variable.


            




    2. Fine data matching and motion estimation: Image Correlation searching in the space of robot's locations.

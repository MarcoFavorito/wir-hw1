Distributed Systems

Research activities

The research activity of the Distributed Systems group
focuses on theoretical aspects, design and performance
analysis of parallel/distributed computing systems.
In particular, the group is interested in the following topics:

  • Theory of distributed computing
  • Highly performing, available Web systems
  • Performance and dependability analysis
  • Parallel/distributed simulation
  • Dependable middleware
  • Mobile systems
  • Communication protocols
  • Interconnection networks

Logistics and Resource Management:

A variety of logistic operations research problems in routing, scheduling, packing, and layout systems will be investigated in cooperation with ORTEC Consultants (a large company specialising in OR applications in industry and government in the Netherlands), Siemens Corporate Research (D) and CCI Europe Inc. (DK). In practical terms, most problems in this area are known to be major challenges in combinatorial optimisation. We shall especially investigate on-line scheduling and vehicle routing problems with constraints. Our research will address feasibility issues and performance guarantees for some of the applicable algorithmic techniques and solutions. The industrial contacts will cooperate with us in the further definition and tuning of the problems.

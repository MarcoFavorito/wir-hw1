Post-Doctoral Fellowship of Fondazione Antonio

Ruberti with the collaboration of EIROforum

 

1 POST-DOCTORAL FELLOWSHIP FOR EUROPEAN CITIZENS IN THE FOLLOWING RESEARCH AREA

 

CULTURAL, SOCIAL AND POLITICAL ASPECTS OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AT THE EUROPEAN LEVEL.

 

The fellowship is intended for young post-doctoral researchers who have not attained 35 years as of October 31, 2004.

 

The fellowship is granted for one year.

 

Candidates should propose EU Universities or not-for-profit Research Institutes as host Institutions.

 

The research program should relate to the research fields of the Department or Laboratory selected and a letter of acceptance must be included.

 

The annual gross salary is EURO 28.645,00.

 

Round trip travel expenses from home country to the EU University or Research Institute will be reimbursed.

 

Candidates should submit their CV, an outline of their proposed project and the names of three referees who have agreed to send reference letters directly to the Foundation.

 

Applications must be sent in electronic format to the Fondazione Antonio Ruberti no later than October 31, 2004.

 

Applications will be evaluated by EIROforum.

 

Candidates will be informed by January 2005 about the decision taken by the EIROforum selection commitee.

 

Fellowship must start in the period from March to May 2005.

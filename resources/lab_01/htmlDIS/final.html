Accepted Papers

The final version of the paper (in ps or pdf format) to be included in informal proceedings distributed at the workshop is due at workshop PC co-chair Roberto Baldoni (baldoni@dis.uniroma1.it) by Dec 6th. The final version should be formatted according to IEEE conference/workshop standard in a two columns format.

The camera-ready copy of each accepted paper will be published in the proceedings and is due at IEEE Computer Society Press office on February 5th, 2000. The detailed instructions for authors will be sent to you directly by IEEE Computer Society Press staff editor by Jan 5th.

Each camera-ready should not exceed eight proceedings pages. Up to two more pages are allowed, provided that the author will pay $150.00 per page beyond the allowable eight pages at the time of submitting the camera-ready copy of the paper.

A note on encoding inverse roles and functional restrictions in ALC knowledge bases

Abstract

In this paper we show that it is possible to eliminate inverse roles and functional restrictions from ALCFI knowledge bases, while preserving the soundness and completeness of inference. Specifically, we present two polynomial encodings, the first from ALCFI knowledge bases into ALCI ones, and the second from ALCI knowledge bases into ALC ones. These encodings eliminate functional restrictions and inverse roles respectively, but add enough information so as not to destroy the meaning of concepts in the original knowledge base with respect to the reasoning tasks.

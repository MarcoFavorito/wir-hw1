Design Methodologies

  • Methodologies for conceptual design are based on:

    • incremental refinements of the schema;
    • abstraction as a refinement mechanism;
    • checking conceptual schema qualities;
    • decomposition in subschemata;
    • integrated analysis of data and functions.

  • Main methodologies are

    • top-down;
    • bottom-up;
    • mixed.

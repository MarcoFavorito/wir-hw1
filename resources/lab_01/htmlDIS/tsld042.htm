From Conceptual to Logical

  • Translation of a conceptual (E-R) schema into a logical (relational) schema.

  • Be careful with integrity constraints!

  • First optimization based on the frequencies of accesses to entities and relationships.

  • Two phases:

    • restructuring of the conceptual schema and first optimization;
    • translation and further optimization (based on the logical model).

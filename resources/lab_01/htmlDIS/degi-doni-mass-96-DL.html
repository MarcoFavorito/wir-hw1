EXPTIME Tableaux for ALC

Francesco M. Donini, Giuseppe De Giacomo, and Fabio Massacci

Proceedings of the 1996 Description Logic Workshop (DL'96)

We propose a tableaux calculus requiring simple exponential time for satisfiability of an ALC concept C wrt a TBox T containing general axioms of the form C v D.

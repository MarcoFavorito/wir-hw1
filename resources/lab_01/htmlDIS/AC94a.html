Heuristic Reasoning in a Real Domain

Marco Adinolfi and Amedeo Cesta

ABSTRACT

The Data Relay Satellite System (DRS) is intended to provide communication services between low earth orbiting satellites and their ground terminals. DRS scheduling is a potentially complex task which would greatly benefit from an optimized resource management. The paper describes a knowledge-based system, DRS-Sched, aimed at solving a noticeable aspect of the DRS scheduling problem. DRSSched combines both predictive and reactive scheduling heuristics in quite an original way.

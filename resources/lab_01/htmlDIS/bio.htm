Biographical sketches

Since November 2002, I am PhD student at the Department of Computer and Systems Science of the University of Rome "La Sapienza".

I earned a Laurea degree in Computer Engineering at University of Rome "La Sapienza" on March 2002 with marks 108/110. The thesis entitled “Active Replication in Three-tier Architectures" has won the national prize “Federcommin-AICA” for best Laurea Thesis in Information Technology on December 2002.

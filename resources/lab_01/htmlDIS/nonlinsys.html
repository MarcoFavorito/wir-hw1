Nonlinear Systems

Research activities

The research in Nonlinear Systems ranges over the following topics: nonlinear regulation, stabilization of nonlinear systems, fault detection for nonlinear systems, dynamic feedback linearization, stochastic stabilization of nonlinear systems, resource management in wireless systems, analysis and control of switched nonlinear systems, control under communication constraints.

RoboCupRescue - Background, History and Objectives

Download the presentation (PDF)

The history of RoboCupRescue started at ICRA98 in Leuven, Belgium, when Hiroaki and Satoshi met at the first time. It has gradually grown up so that both robot league and simulation league have many participants now. This talk introduces the background and the history of RoboCupRescue, and consideres its objectives.

Scientific Curriculum of Fausto Giunchiglia

For more detailed information, see Fausto Giunchiglia's web page

Fausto Giunchiglia is associate professor in Computer Science at the University of Trento and leader of a research group at IRST (Istituto per la Ricerca Scientifica e Tecnologica) in Trento. He is in the "Advisory Board" of the "Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research" (JAIR). He is one of the editors of the Journal on Software Tools for Technology Transfer (STTT) and of the "Special Issue on Agency" of the "Journal of Logic and Computation (JLC)". He is programme commitee member of more than 20 international conferences and workshops, such as AAAI, KR, IJCAI, ECAI, CADE, ATAL, TACAS. He published on the Journal of Artificial Intelligence, IEEE proceedings, Journal of Automated Reasoning, Information and Computation, Internation Journal of Intelligent Systems, IJCAI, ECAI.

ICARUS: Intelligent Classification And Retrieval of Unlabelled Scenes

Emilio Domenicucci, Francesco M. Donini, and Marco Schaerf

Proceedings of the 1999 Description Logic Workshop (DL'99)

In this paper we present ICARUS: a tool for the creation, classification and retrieval of VRML scenes. ICARUS classifies each scene using a concept description language and uses its reasoning mechanism to retrieve information.

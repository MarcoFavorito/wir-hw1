A framework for branch-and-cut algorithms (ABACUS):

Branch-and-cut is the most successful paradigm for solving NP-hard optimisation problems to optimality. Despite its proven effectiveness, it is not yet widely used. A preliminary study has shown that a major part of a typical branch-and-cut code is generic, i.e., common to all such codes. We shall cast the generic part into a framework (ABACUS), potentially allowing considerable savings in the implementation effort for every new branch-and-cut algorithm.

Graph Drawing in Software Systems:

We shall work on problems supplied by EDS Database Informatica (I), the Netherlands Bureau for Statistics (NL), and Dassault (F) in the development of graph-drawing facilities. We shall analyse tools for relevant classes of diagrams (statistical, entity-relationship, data-flow, PERT, state-transition etc). We shall focus on the management of large diagrams, diagram integration, diagram design by using refinement primitives, maintenance of consistency between text-based and diagram-based documentation, automatic recognition of similar parts, and reverse-engineering. Furthermore, we shall use the test cases to improve the reliability of existing graph-drawing platforms and to test their effectiveness in real-life graph-drawing-intensive applications.

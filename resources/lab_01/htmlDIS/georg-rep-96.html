Expectation Relations: A Uniform Approach to Nonmonotonicity

Konstantinos Georgatos

Technical Report, Dipartimento di Informatica e Sistemistica, Università di Roma "La Sapienza"

We show that Gabbay's nonmonotonic consequence relations can be reduced to a new family of relations based on the notion of expectation. Expectation relations provide a direct generalization of epistemic entrenchment and expectation ordering introduced by Gärdenfors and Makinson for the study of expectation inference and belief revision, respectively.

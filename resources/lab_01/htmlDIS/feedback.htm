WORKSHOP on
LARGE SCALE NONLINEAR OPTIMIZATION
June 22 - July 1, 2004
Erice, Italy
 







 

 

How to participate

 

Persons wishing to attend the Workshop should apply sending the application form to the e-mail address erice2004@dis.uniroma1.it   before April 30, 2004. 

If they wish to contribute a lecture (30 min.), they should also include title and abstract of the proposed lecture.

A limited number of places is available. If necessary, admission to the Workshop will be decided by the Advisory Committee of the School composed of Professors G. Di Pillo, F. Giannessi and M. Roma. Admission will be communicated shortly after the closing date for application.

 

Detailed information

ARRIVAL/DEPARTURE

The easiest way to reach Erice is either via Palermo or Trapani. Then the "E. Majorana" Centre will provide transportation to Erice from Palermo or Trapani airports, seaports or railway stations. Participants are expected to arrive and to leave in the following days:

Arrival day:  June 22 - Departure day: July 1, 2004

 

LODGING:

Participants will be located by the Centre. Since Erice is a small, ancient village, the number of rooms is limited and the Centre kindly ask participants if they can share a room.

 

FEE:

The total fee is Euro 900,00. It includes lodging, breakfast, lunch, dinner, coffee breaks, one day excursion, social dinner and other social events, transportation from Palermo or Trapani airport/railway stations to Erice on June 22, and vice versa on July 1.  Single room requires an extra charge of Euro 100,00.

 

ACCOMPANYING PERSONS:

The fee for accompanying persons is Euro 450,00 per person, if the accompanying person shares the room of the participant; Euro 900,00 otherwise.

 

PAYMENT:

The total amount must be paid to Centro "E. Majorana" :

either by cash on the arrival (no credit cards),

or by bank transfer to:

Centro "E. Majorana"  -  c/o Banco di Sicilia, Agenzia Erice Vetta (phone:+39-0923-869615) Account No. 5110, coordinates of the Bank: ABI 01020, CAB 81850, writing explicitly that the payment is related to the 40th Workshop of Mathematics.

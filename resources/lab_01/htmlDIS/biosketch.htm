I was born in Rome, January 9th 1978. I received the "Laurea" degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2001 from the University of Rome "La Sapienza" and the Master MSc (Eng) in Control Systems in 2003 from the University of Sheffield, U.K. I am currently a Ph.D. student in Systems Engineering at the Dipartimento di Informatica e Sistemistica (DIS) of the University of Rome "La Sapienza".
You can find me at the DIS Robotics Laboratory.

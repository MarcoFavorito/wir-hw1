Computing Nash Equilibria for Scheduling on Restricted Parallel Links
Burkhard Monien
University of Paderborn

DIS - Dipartimento di Informatica e Sistemistica, via Salaria 113
Aula C2, piano secondo

 

 

Abstract:
We consider the problem of routing n users on m parallel links, under the restriction that each user may only be routed on a link from a certain set of allowed links for the user. In a pure Nash Equilibrium, no user may improve its own individual cost (delay) by unilaterally switching to another link. As our main result, we introduce a polynomial time algorithm to compute from any given assignment a pure Nash Equilibrium with non increased congestion.

(Joint work with Martin Gairing, Thomas Luecking and Marios Mavronicolas)

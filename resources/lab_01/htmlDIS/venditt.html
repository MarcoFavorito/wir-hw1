Bio Sketch

I received the ``Laurea" degree in Electrical Engineering in 1992 and the Ph.D. in Systems Engineering in 1997, both from the Università degli Studi di Roma ``La Sapienza". From April 1995 to January 1996 I was a visiting scholar at LAAS-CNRS in Toulouse (France), where I held a post-doc position from April 1997 to October 1998 funded by the EC through a TMR grant. I am currently Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer and Systems Science (DIS) of the University of Rome ``La Sapienza" and I am a member of the Robotics Laboratory.

Research interests

My main research interests are in the area of  robot motion planning and control.

The Importance of Being Ranked Or How Algebra and Probability Can Help You Surf Better
Prof. Devdatt Dubhashi
Computer Science, Chalmers and Gothenborg University

Dipartimento di Informatica e Sistemistica - DIS
via Salaria 113, piano secondo
Aula C2

Abstract:
Web surfing has now become everyone's favourite pastime. More seriously, the Web is now increasingly becoming the source of information for all things ranging from scientific articles to tourist holidays. However, its heterogeneity and lack of standards means that the task of getting good information from the Web is non-trivial. How do (some) search engines deliver good results? The goal of the talk will be to convince you that in a real world scenario like this, progress is made not by mindless hacking but by applying "serious" theory from Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science.

Stereo Vision

Robotic systems are becoming smaller, lower power, and cheaper, enabling their application in areas not previously considered. This is true of vision systems as well. SRI's Small Vision Module (SVM) is a compact, inexpensive realtime device for computing dense stereo range images, which are a fundamental measurement supporting a wide range of computer vision applications. In this talk, I will give an overview of hardware and software issues in the construction of the SVM, and describe several ongoing projects that take advantage of stereo processing, including realtime people-tracking.

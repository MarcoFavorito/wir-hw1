Research activities

The research activities of the group are oriented mainly in three directions:

1) Theoretical and application-oriented aspects of visual formalisms for databases and database design;
2) Design and performance modelling of parallel and distributed database systems;
3) Database modeling, cooperative information systems, data integration, data warehouses and data mining, semi-structured data modeling.

The main research topics are: Visual Query Languages and Interfaces, Databases and the Web, Heterogeneous and Cooperative Database Systems, 2D and 3D Data Visualization, Adaptive Interfaces, Visual Metaphors, Controlled Studies and Usability Testing, Query Language Expressive Power, Data Modeling, Data Warehousing, Parallel Database Systems, Query Optimization, Workflow Management Systems, Semi-structured Data, Data Integration.

Besides several publications in international journals and conferences, the group has also designed and implemented several prototypes of visual interfaces for database access, such as QBD* and QBI, which have been transformed into industrial products by an italian software company.

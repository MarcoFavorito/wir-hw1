Biography

Marco Pirrone obtained his Master Thesis in Computer Engineering in July 1999 with  sum cum laude. From October 1999 he has been studying  as PhD Student in University of Rome "La Sapienza". He is working on artificial intelligence and robot vision. During the period spent in the  "Dipartimento di Informatica e Sistemistica" he has studied the evolutes concepts of robot vision and artificial  vision, in particular he has concentrated his effort on "visual attention", starting from the low  level to the high level  vision, analyzing context free and context sensitive approach.  He obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering in November 2003.

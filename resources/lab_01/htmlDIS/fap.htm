Successful Applications to GSM networks

The approach presented in Solving Stability Problems on a Superclass of Interval Graphs is indeed very successful in solving very large real-life instances of frequency assignment in GSM networks (in Germany). A complete list of results and benchmarks is available at   FAP web. Our approach also requires the implementation of a Simulated Annealing procedure, briefly described here.

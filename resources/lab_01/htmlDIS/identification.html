Identification and Optimal Control

 

Research activities

 

The scientific interest of the group includes the following topics:

  • Modelling, identification and filtering for discontinuous 2D signals, with reference to the problem of edges detection and motion estimation from sequences of  blurred and noisy images.
  • Application of modelling, filtering and optimal control methodologies to traffic control in wireless communication networks.
  • Population-based global optimization procedures with possible applications.
  • Modelling and identification of  biomedical systems.

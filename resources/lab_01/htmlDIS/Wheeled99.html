Results of ART in RoboCup-99

The competition has been attended by 21 teams from USA, Japan, Australia, Germany, France, Iran, Singapore, Italy and Portugal. ART took the second place in the preliminary round, winning against Osaka (Japan), SharifCE (Iran), USC (USA), Minho (Portogallo), drawing with Monaco (Germany) and losing with Stoccarda (Germany). ART won the quarter-finals 3-0 against Ulm (Germany) and the semi-final against CS Freiburg (Germany), current champion, at the final challenge after a draw 0-0 at the regular time and 1-1 at the penalty kicks. In the final match ART has bee defeated 3-1 by SharifCE (Iran), thus reaching the second place in the championship.

The main issue of interest of ART-99 has been the capability of coordination among the robots in the team, that have been developed both in hardware and in software independently and heterogeneously by the different Italian Universities associated in ART. This feature has been an innovative research issue since it has represented a concrete application of cooperation among heterogeneous mobile robots performing a complex task in a dynamic environment. Moreover, the coordination mechanism allowed us to define different game strategies depending on the different situations occurring.

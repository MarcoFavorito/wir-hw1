ROUTING PROTOCOL "DYNAMIC SOURCE ROUTING"

The routing protocol DSR "Dynamic Source Routing" appertains at family of Reactive protocol. In a Manet network the creation of route only On-Demand, is certainly more practicable, as to uninterrupted exchange of informations at intervals of fixed time.

The second point is that the protocol DSR, as to all prensent existed protocols, this offers two different alternation for to guarantee the search of a route.

These phase are:
   
Search the Route in route cache (the route cache is a table where the route for to arrive to device destination, are strored in fixed times); this method offers a remarkable lowering of packets the traffic on network.
   
Route Discovery

is a phase where one device discover all his neighbours (Discovery Neighbour) and then search a route for to arrive a destination device. This method allows a device to communicate whit another device that don't stay in the same trasmission range (the maxim distance, up to today, is about 35 metre).

 

The third point is that DSR is very appropriate for the network where the number of device is about fifty. Infact the scenary of use is the manet network where the devices that compose her are maxim twenty devices.

But the most important ground is the maintenance (for all devices) of only one table (the name of table is route cache). The route cache maintains all required informations for to arrive at destination device.

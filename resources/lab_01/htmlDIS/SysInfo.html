ALCOM-IT Project

Project Objectives

  • Development of technologies and methods for the design of efficient algorithms for sequential, parallel and distributed computing
  • Improvement in the understanding of efficient computing
  • Delivery of correct, efficient, and easy-to-use libraries of combinatorial software of general applicability
  • Solutions to efficiency-critical algorithmic and optimisation problems arising in particular IT application domains
    • Management of large computer and communication networks
    • Advanced graphical software engineering environments
    • Logistics and resource management
  • Transfer to application developers, in industry and elsewhere

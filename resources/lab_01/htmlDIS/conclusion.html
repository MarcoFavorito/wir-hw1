Conclusions



    Stereo vision is a good sensor for mobile robots.

    Reliable localization and 3D map building is possible with a calibrated stereo vision system.



    3D Map building of office-like environment

    • High-quality reconstruction
    • Limited human intervention
    • Perspective errors for objects not on the wall

    Large Baseline Camera Motion Estimation

    • Accurate motion estimation
    • Accurate and robust object reconstruction
    • Large search space


    General limitation of the approach: the views should be not too far from a standard setting

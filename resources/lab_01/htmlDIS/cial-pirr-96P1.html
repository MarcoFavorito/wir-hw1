A Study on the logic of Abduction

Marta Cialdea and Fiora Pirri

Proceedings of the Twelfth European Conference on Artificial Intelligence (ECAI'96)

In this paper we present a logic for abduction, namely a language, a semantics and a proof theory where the abductive relation can be expressed. The abductive relation is the relation between a preferred sentence explainig a given observation, in the context of a background theory, and such an observation itself. The proposed logical system, based on a modal approach, captures propositional abduction over a finite language.

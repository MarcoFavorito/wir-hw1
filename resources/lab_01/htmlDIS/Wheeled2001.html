The Italian national team ART was not active anymore and University "La Sapienza" has developed two teams: SPQR-Legged and SPQR-Wheeled partecipating at:

  • GermanOpen 2001 (Paderborn, Giugno 2001)
  • Robocup-01 (Seattle, Agosto 2001)
  • RoboCup Camp - Parigi (Paris, April 2001)

 

Daniele Nardi and Ryad Benossman organized the second RoboCup Camp in Paris (April 2001).

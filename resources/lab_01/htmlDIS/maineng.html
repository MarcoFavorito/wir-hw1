Fifth Congress of the Italian Association for Artificial Intelligence (AI*IA'97)

Roma September 16-19, 1997




The Fifth Congress of the Italian Association for Artificial Intelligence will be held in Roma, Facoltà di Ingegneria of the Università ``La Sapienza'', on September 16-19, 1997. Previous conferences have been held in Florence (1995), Turin (1993), Palermo (1991) and Trento (1989).



The language of the Congress is English. The Congress includes the presentation of 37 Technical Papers, two invited lectures, four tutorials and two Workshops.
